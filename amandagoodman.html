Amanda L. Goodman

Partner

Education

Amanda Leigh Goodman earned a B.A. from Davidson College in Davidson, North Carolina, in 2010, and a JD (with honors) from Florida State University in 2013.

Memberships and Activities

She is admitted to practice law in Florida and is a member of the Young Lawyers, Tax Law, and Real Property, Probate & Trust Law sections of the Florida Bar.

In addition, Amanda belongs to the Collier County Bar Association. She has practiced with Goodman Breen & Gibbs for three years.
