Kenneth D. Goodman

Partner

Education

Kenneth D. Goodman received a BBA from Oklahoma State University in Stillwater, Oklahoma and a JD (with honors) from the University of Oklahoma.

Memberships and Activities

He is admitted to practice law in Florida and Texas. In addition, he is a Certified Public Accountant.

Among other professional organizations, he has served on the International Estate Planning Subcommittee of the Estate & Gift Taxes Committee, Section of Taxation, American Bar Association and on various subcommittees for the Real Property, Probate & Trust Section of the American Bar Association.

Kenny also belongs to the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and has served on the International Taxation Subcommittee for the Florida Institute of Certified Public Accountants.

Kenny has authored articles in various professional journals, including Trusts & Estates and Estate Planning.

Kenny is recognized by the Florida Bar as a Board Certified Wills, Trusts and Estates Lawyer.
