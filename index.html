About Goodman Breen

At Goodman Breen, we concentrate our practice on estate and trust related matters. Our attorneys, Kenneth D. Goodman, Dorothy M. Breen and Amanda Leigh Goodman, have a wide range of experience in these areas. Kenny, Dorothy, and Amanda are committed to providing quality legal advice and service. Our primary areas of practice are described below.

Services

Estate Planning

We do a substantial amount of estate planning. The nature of the plans we prepare for our clients traverse a broad spectrum. From the simplest of estate plans where estate taxes are not an issue, to tax efficient plans for married couples, to generation-skipping planning, to charitable giving planning, to life insurance trusts, we have assisted our clients in addressing their needs and goals. We have also dealt with closely-held business issues in the estate planning context.

Estate and Trust Administration

We have significant experience in assisting with the administration of estates and trusts, both in advising personal representatives and trustees, and assisting beneficiaries regarding their rights and expectations. Our attorneys have been involved in preparing estate tax returns and dealing with the IRS on various tax-related issues, including valuation disputes and significant legal issues. Our staff is also very experienced in probate and trust matters, allowing us to accomplish the necessary tasks efficiently.

Income Tax Matters

Our attorneys also deal with disputes with the IRS regarding income taxes for individuals, trusts and estates and closely-held businesses. Our attorneys also prepare income tax returns for a significant number of clients.
